The Weekend 29th-30th November in Florence


This weekend Florence embraces the festive season with Christmas markets and winter magic,
while hosting the 41st Florence Marathon and celebrating Japanese culture.
From ice skating to vintage treasures, from natural wines to running through art-filled streets,
the weekend offers sport, culture, and seasonal delights.
Get ready to explore, discover, and enjoy!



Christmas Market at Piazza Santa Croce

Discover the magic of Christmas in Piazza Santa Croce with the Weihnachtsmarkt, the traditional German Christmas market.
Every day you'll find stalls with crafts, typical products, gift ideas, Christmas decorations, and European food.

When: 10 AM - 10/11 PM



Florence on Ice

The Giardino della Fortezza da Basso turns into a Christmas Village
with the longest ice-skating rink in Europe and a 55-meter Ferris wheel offering a stunning view over Florence's skyline.

When: 10 AM - 3 PM / 8 PM



Florence Marathon - Sunday 30th November

A perfect chance to discover Florence all in one go---kilometers of art-filled corners and historic squares,
fast stretches along the Arno, cheering crowds, and that unique charm born from centuries of creativity.
A real dream to experience at the 41st Estra Florence Marathon this Sunday.



Comic, Record & Vintage Market

Piazza dei Ciompi hosts a weekend market full of retro vibes---
unmissable for fans of collecting and timeless style.

When: 9 AM - 8 PM



Vinoi - Artisanal & Natural Wine Fair & Fest

At the Visarno racetrack, a cool independent event
dedicated to artisanal, organic, biodynamic, and natural wines.

When: 11 AM - 8 PM



Empoli Christmas Town

The historic center lights up with Christmas decorations, unique attractions, and 70 wooden market huts!
This weekend Piazza G. Guerra also features Passione Vintage e Handmade,
another market focused on vintage pieces and high-quality crafts.

When: 10 AM - 7 PM



Wasshoi Manifattura

At Manifattura Tabacchi, an event celebrating Japanese culture in all its forms,
with music, art, dance, and food.



Antiques Market - Piazza Annigoni

Piazza Annigoni fills with stands offering antiques, collectibles, books, paintings,
and all sorts of vintage treasures.



Collecting in Florence - Saturday 29th November

On Saturday, the parking area of the former Tuscany Hall hosts a market
dedicated to collectibles, small antiques, modern antiques, vintage items, paper curiosities, and creative crafts.

When: 10 AM - 7 PM



Only Usato Market - Sunday 30th November

At Le Murate, the event dedicated to second-hand culture
and vintage fashion is back.

When: 11 AM - 7 PM



Markets Around Florence

On Sunday there's a craft market in Piazza Mino in Fiesole;
also on Sunday, the Mercatino del Viandante Christmas edition will take place in the cloister of the Pieve di San Pietro a Ripoli.
There will also be Christmas markets in Badia a Settimo and San Godenzo.
And don't miss "Waiting for Christmas" in Signa!
